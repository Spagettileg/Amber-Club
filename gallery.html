Connemara Peated

Price £37.75

Tasting Notes

Appearance - Clear, mid golden amber with polished golden glints.

Aroma - Sweet barley with wafts of peaty smoke, kippers and well baked apple crumble.

Taste - Lightly honey sweet, subdued clean rather than sooty smoke with peaty vegetal notes, sweet barley water, light spice and vanilla oak.

Kilbeggan Traditional

Price £21.45

Tasting Notes

Appearance - Clear, mid golden amber with golden glow.

Aroma - Traces of sweet oloroso with sweet malt that overpowers the clean grain.

Taste - The malt moves freely around the palate picking up sweet honey notes. The grain comes into play a little later to form a smooth middle.

Dubliner

Price £22.95

Tasting Notes

Appearance - Clear, slight golden amber with misty golden glow.

Aroma - Traces of sweet walnut with dry pepper that overpowers the clean grain.

Taste - The walnut is slight and moves freely around the palate picking up dry pepper notes. The grain comes into play a little later to form a smooth end taste.
