Cookies Information

How cookies affect you...

"Cookies" are small pieces of information sent by a web server to a web browser, which enables the server to collect information from the browser. You can find out more about the way cookies work on http://www.cookiecentral.com. Re-Boot uses cookies for a number of purposes, for instance to enable us to simplify the logging on process for registered users, to help ensure the security and authenticity of registered users, to provide the mechanisms for online shopping and to enable traffic monitoring.

Non-registered visitors of the site are sent anonymous cookies to keep track of their browsing patterns and build up a demographic profile.

Whilst you do not need to allow your browser to accept cookies in order to browse much of our web site or to access many of our services, you must have cookies enabled if you wish to shop online or access any areas reserved for registered users.

FAQ Information

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Whiskey-Drop free to join?

No, there is a joining fee payable of £25 to join Whiskey-Drop. All funds help the maintenance of our website and support a minimal income to our expert writers through canvassing their deep knowledge on the wonderful products made available through Whiskey-Drop.

How does Whiskey-Drop work?

The worlds major whiskey distilleries are in regular contact with Whiskey-Drop to recommend their products for retail. This 'commercial goodwill' allows Whiskey-Drop to sell discounted high quality products to our signed up members, together with upto 6 free samples per year. Registered members simply browse the stock, request a free sample of their choosing, if they're unable to decide on a particular purchase. Formal purchases are made directly through Whiskey-Drop. Members experiences can be captured via the various Whiskey-Drop social media links. There is a 42 day cooling off period if customers decide an unopened purchase was made in error. Full refunds will be payable in the same period only.

The general public can view the image, price and tasting notes of all whiskey products in stock.

All users will need to register by uploading their personal details and then enter their login credentials.

Whiskey-Drop products can be viewed by the detailed listing or the user can use the 'Search' facility to locate a specific whiskey offering. All selected whiskies will then be captured into the shopping cart. Upon clicking the shopping cart icon (positioned at the top of the page), the registered user will proceed to a formal checkout process until they receive formal confirmation of a sale.

What controls exist on whiskey quality?

All Whiskey-Drop products are sourced directly from major international drinks distilleries and are subject to the highest levels of scrutiny and quality control.

Why dont I receive a refund for my unwanted purchase?

42 calendar days are given to all registered members to decide on a unwanted product return and refund payment to be processed within this period. It should be noted that 7 calendar days are required to process a payment refund. If the 42nd day expires, then Whiskey-Drop reserves the right to not proceed with a refund. The onus is on the registered member to act swiftly to request a refund, subject to this being required.

Who receives the subscription money?

Whiskey-Drop receives the money and funds are both reinvested into the maintenance of the website and covering costs of our product writing experts too. No profit is generated via the subscription fee transactions.

What countries does Whiskey-Drop operate in?

Whiskey-Drop is a global enterprise meaning we trade in 120 countries. If you're unsure you're country is included, please contact us to find out if we can trade with you.

Who is responsible for the postal / shipping costs of the whiskey?

Postal costs are bourne by the registered member, with the price determined by location of destination. Registered postal service will be used.

Who is able to provide reviews and feedback?

Only registered users can provide reviews / feeback on their 'Whiskey-Drop' experience.
