Heres our story...

Whiskey-Drop

Founded in 2018, Whiskey-Drop is already establishing itself as a highly reputable purveyor and knowledge base for fine whiskies. Our credentials of listening to what our customers really want, sourcing the globe for new and existing products and ensuring rapid delivery of ordered products make for a rewarding and successful business.
Customers are encouraged to sample our products 'free of charge' in order to make sure the product(s) ordered are right in terms of taste and affordability.
For a nominal membership fee, our members can expect access to the most amazing whiskey products that exist on our planet today, discounts on selected products, free introduction gift and an opportunity to contact with our in-house professionals for advice on our whiskey products.

This is thirsty work...

Sample Before You Buy

With so much choice on offer, knowing where to start can be such a daunting experience. Whiskey-Drop repeat customers enjoy 'free samples' of whiskies they are curious about, but not sure of making a financial committment for a complete product.
As a fully signed up member, you will be entitled to 6 free samples per year. This means that your hard earned money will not go to waste and you'll get the opportunity to extend your taste vocabulary on new products.
Whiskey-Drop would love to hear of your tasting experiences through either contacting us direct or leave a message via our social media links visible at the bottom of the page.

A Fine Collection

We can boast to having access to the broadest collection of whiskies with copious amounts of quality per single drop contained in every bottle sourced and sold.
Our members can purchase in single bottle quantities or by the case. We're here to serve you.
Most of the worlds major distilleries are in regular contact to ensure their premium products are made available, together with accurate tasting notes to ensure our members are fully informed of our products and to avoid any surprises during the ordering process.
So, if you savour peaty notes, sherry cask seasoning, fruity sweetness or something more spicy, then our much vaunted collection of the finest whiskies will be of great interest to you.
